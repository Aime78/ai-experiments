This module manages all **client identity information** for individuals and organizations opening accounts.

## 1.1 Personal Identification Information

These informations are used for opening **individual accounts**.

Required fields for an individual client:

- **Last Name**
- **Middle Name (Post Name)**
- **First Name**
- **Gender**
- **Nationality**
- **Date of Birth**
- **Place of Birth**
- **Marital Status**
    - Single
    - Married
    - Divorced
- **Profession**
    - Trader
    - Student
    - Employee
    - Other
- **Province of Origin**

### Identification Details

- **Type of Identification Document**
    - National ID Card
    - CEPGL Card
    - Passport
- **Identification Number**

### Address information

- **Province**
- **Commune**
- **Neighborhood (Quartier)**
- **Avenue**
- **Plot Number**

Optional:

- **Email Address**

Required:

- **Phone Number**

## 1.2. Mandataire (Authorized Representative) and optional

A **Mandataire** is a person authorized to operate the account.

This is **optional** except for certain organizational accounts.

Required Information:

- Last Name
- Middle Name
- First Name
- Identification document copy

## 1.3. Minor Account Responsibility

If the account holder is a **minor**, a responsible adult must be provided.

Required information:

- Full name
- Copy of identification document

## 1.4. Required Documents

- National ID Card / Passport / CEPGL Card
- Two passport photos

## 1.5 Biometric Verification

The client must provide:

- **Signature**
    
    OR
    
- **Fingerprint**

## 1.2 Business Identification information

These informations are used to open accounts for **companies, schools, or organizations**.

### Required Information

- **Account Holder**
    - Name of the company or organization
- **Account Opening Request Letter**
    - Must include the names of **authorized representatives (mandataires)**

### Authorized Representative Information

For each representative:

- Last Name
- Middle Name
- First Name
- Gender
- Identification Type
- Identification Number
- Address
- Profession
- Place and Date of Birth
- Province of Origin
- Marital Status
- Role in the Company (Observation)

### Address

- Neighborhood (Quartier)
- Avenue
- Commune
- Plot Number

### Biometric Verification

- **Signature**
    
    OR
    
- **Fingerprint**

### Signature Requirements

There are two types of signatures:

### Mandatory Signatures

These signatures are **required to validate account operations**.

Example:

- Primary account holder

---

### Optional Signatures

These signatures are **only used if additional control is required**.

Example:

- Secondary representative
- Co-signatory

## Business or Organization Accounts

- Official registration documents:

Examples:

- **Ministerial decree** (for schools)
- **Notarized company statutes**
- **Business registration certificate**
